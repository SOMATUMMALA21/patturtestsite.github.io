Bay Area Older Adults is a...

Charitable 501(c)(3) organization that offers outdoor, social and cultural activities for adults age 50+. Membership is FREE and gives you member–only discounts, special offers and a monthly newsletter of group events. Click 'Join' in the navigation bar for more information regarding registration. We offer two programs: a 'Do-It-Yourself Adventures' Program in which members can scroll through an assortment of activities and a 'Group Adventures' program where we'll explore various subjects with you!
