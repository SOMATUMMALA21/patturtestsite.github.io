How It Works

1. For round 1, you submit a 5 minute video along with answering some questions about your product/service.

2. Our esteemed teens, along with a panel of professional entrepreneurs, judge your pitches using the criteria listed below.

3. The top 10 teams then pitch live in front of a panel of experienced judges from around Silicon Valley.

Prizes

1st Place: $500

2nd Place: $400

3rd Place: $200

*All 3 teams will get help filling a patient. They will also all become semifinalists in Blaze Global, where they will compete for 15k

Rubric

Round 1 Criteria

Feasibility (25%) How much traction has this team had? Is this project technically practical? Given the skills that the team possesses, is this project feasible?

Innovation (25%) How unique is this team’s idea? Has this been done before?

Flammability (10%) How will the $6,000 grand prize be used to further their business? How likely is it that the $6,000 will accelerate the business?

Market Research and Growth Potential (25%) Does the team have a thorough understanding of the market and competitive landscape? How large is the market they are trying to reach? Has the team done customer research?

Presentation Quality (15%) How compelling is the presentation/video? Does it wow the judges?
