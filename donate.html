Individual Donations

If you would like to support us financially, there are two ways to do so.

BY MAIL: Please send contributions to Bay Area Older Adults, 4750 Almaden Expressway Ste 124-125, San Jose, CA 95118.

ONLINE: To donate online with a major credit card or PayPal, please click the donate button below.

We appreciate your support. Gifts of any amount are appreciated and will help to fund our Healthy Living™ Program that includes physical activity, lifelong learning and socialization programs. We will provide a formal letter via email and/or regular mail for you to use for tax purposes. For more information, please email info@bayareaolderadults.org
