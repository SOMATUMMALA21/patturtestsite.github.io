What We Do


The Climate Youth Ambassadors are a network of environmentally-conscious teenagers from across the Bay Area who work together to raise awareness about the significance of climate change and equip individuals with the knowledge and resources they need to lead more environmentally-friendly lifestyles.

Our Activities


We are present at community events all across the Bay Area, and we present directly to various elementary and middle schools in the Bay Area as well.

Community Events

We present and set up booths at lots of community events in the Bay Area! Our booths contain lots of engaging activities, and we give away lots of prizes too!

Hidden
Presentations and Assemblies

We reach out to elementary and middle schools in the Bay Area and conduct assemblies that educate students about the effects of climate change and global warming, as well as what they can do to reduce their carbon footprint and adopt more environmentally-friendly habits.
